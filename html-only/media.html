BRANDING

Logo

Our logo combines many different team elements; the L&N sign featured hangs on the corner of our school, the gryphon in the center is our school's mascot, the gears at the top emphasize that we are a robotics team, 3966 is our team number, and we are the "STEMpunks."

Standard - Only Green - Only White

Fonts

When creating posters, apparel, or other brand elements, our team utilizes the Oud Warenhuis and Oxygen fonts. We picked the Oud Warenhuis font because it reflects steampunk design ethics, is easily legible at a distance, and includes a secondary "shadow." Oxygen serves as our secondary font for large blocks of small text, as it is more legible than Oud Warenhuis at small sizes.

Oud Warenhuis - Oxygen

Slogan / Tagline

Our slogan, "Get STEMpunked," is commonly shown in brand elements that accompany our logo. The slogan is typically depicted in the Oud Warenhuis font.

White - Green - White (No Shadow) - Green (No Shadow)

Colors

Our team utilizes three colors in our branding; green, white, and gold. Our primary and tertiary colors, green and gold, are derived from our school's colors. We use white as a secondary color due in part to it's easiness to view and cost efficiency in printing.

Green - #0a6118 White - #ffffff Gold - #c37417

Mascot

Our mascot, the gryphon, is shared with our school. We use the gryphon on our shirts and banners and as an accompanying element in our designs on our products.
