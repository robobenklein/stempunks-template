Introducing: Calypstote

After six weeks of hard work, our team finished our robot for the 2015 competition, Recycle Rush. Calypstote will compete at the Smoky Mountains Regional in downtown Knoxville, Tennessee at the Knoxville Convention Center.

The L&N STEMpunks

We're students from the L&N STEM Academy with a passion to learn and innovate. We work to spread STEM education and FIRST programs throughout the East Tennessee region. Although the FIRST Robotics Competition is about building a robot, many of our members specialize in other fields such as design, business management, media production, and programming.

L&N STEM Academy

We're located in the heart of downtown Knoxville, Tennessee at the L&N STEM Academy, the 12th best high school in the state. We're a short walk away from the iconic Sunsphere and Knoxville Convention Center, where the Smoky Mountain Regional is held.
